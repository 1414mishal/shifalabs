Common Lab Questions

How should I prepare for an allergy test?

Preparation depends on the type of test. For blood-based allergy tests, usually no special preparation is needed. However, you should inform us about any anti-allergy medications you are currently taking.

Do I need to fast for my blood test?

For tests like Fasting Blood Sugar or Lipid Profile, a 10-12 hour fast is required. For routine allergy panels, fasting is usually not mandatory.

How do I book a Home Collection?

You can book a home collection by clicking our WhatsApp button or calling us. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for early morning slots.

When will I get my results?

Routine blood results are usually available the same evening. Specialized allergy research panels may take 2-3 working days for accurate analysis.
